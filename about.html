About VITCH

Our Story

VITCH was created on April 13, 2026, by a passionate group of developers dedicated to revolutionizing digital payments in Africa. Founded and led by Melkisédeck DJIMADJO, our mission is to simplify and secure financial transactions across the continent.

Our Mission

To empower Africans with innovative fintech solutions that are accessible, secure, and efficient. We believe in bridging the gap between traditional banking and modern digital needs.

Our Vision

To become the leading fintech platform in Africa, providing virtual cards, online payments, money transfers, and integrated services that foster financial inclusion and growth.
